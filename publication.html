Research Paper Publications

We try to provide the developers the chance to experiment on developing games using different algorithms and architectures. Furthermore, we hope to provide performance analysis on different architectures and introduce optimal architectures. There are lots of room to improve, specially learning from parallel programming paradigms. There will be more research paper publications about MMOG and it's performance.

This is an undergraduate project of University of Moratuwa.
